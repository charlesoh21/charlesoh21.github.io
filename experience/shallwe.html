ShallWe

  

The Product (In development)

ShallWe is a mobile app designed to ease the complexities of the planning process, creating personalized hangout schedules for users.

The Problem

With a plethora of choices, individuals are often overwhelmed, spending excessive time planning the outing, whether it's a date, a casual hangout, or a travel adventure.

The Solution

By integrating OpenAI and Yelp APIs, ShallWe delivers tailored hangout schedules in an instant. Beyond mere suggestions, ShallWe turns the planning process into a fun journey in itself.

Target Customer

Students & early professionals in metropolitan cities
