    TL;DR

    imagiCal is a UC Berkeley’s award-winning creative strategy agency. As Berkeley's chapter of the American Advertising Federation, we engage in consulting projects and compete against over 200 colleges in the National Student Advertising Compeition (NSAC). 

    Meta Quest, our 2022 NSAC client, tasked us to create a marketing campaign that inspired brand love among Gen-Z. Our team performed primary & secondary research and created experiential executions with hi-fi mockups.

    We placed 3rd in the nation, our best finish in school history since 1980.

    The Ask

    A fully-integrated, multimillion-dollar marketing campaign to establish VR as a college essential.

    Primary Research

    Methodology: User surveys and one-to-one interviews to extract Gen-Z's perception of VR and generate customer personas



    Secondary Research

    Methodology: Analysis of product success cases in other industries to understand how they have created a sense of community for their customers



    Customer Segmentation

    Methodology: Synthesis of primary and secondary research to define customer segments and their needs



    Example Mockups

    Design Strategy: Celebration of the enhancement of everyday life, offering a chance for users to experience life in full color
