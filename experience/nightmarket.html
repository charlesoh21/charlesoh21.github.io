Nightmarket

  

The Company

Inspired by Asian night markets - accessible, cultural places where individuals gather to exchange goods - Nightmarket provides a similar platform to connect borrowers and lenders of exclusive, high-end streetwear.


Orange Planet Incubator S18 Batch


The Problem

1. Streetwear brands limit their supply of clothes, resulting in overinflated price points in the resale market that limit access to the general public.

2. The fashion industry is notorious for its waste problem; streetwear clothing is constantly being put out of use when new items release.

The Solution

By borrowing instead of buying, consumers gain much more accessibility to varieties of fashion items while staying within budget. Lenders can also profit from lending out items they have no use for or have yet to resell. Lastly, by partaking in the sharing economy, we are advocates of sustainability as we prevent non-degradable materials from going into landfills, one by one.

The Mission

Our mission is trifold- to offer and promote affordability, accessibility, and sustainability for anyone who wants to enjoy streetwear.


Target Customer

High school & college students in the Bay Area
